Welcome!

Hello, and welcome to Caleb's Campaign Hub! Here you will be able to explore the worlds and stories that are being woven into one great universe. Hover your cursor toward the top of the screen to see the menu. Navigate to view information sorted by timeline or campaign through their respective tabs, or by topic through the Encyclopedia.

In the timeline view, you will see an overview of the campaigns and how they have effected each other. For a more specific detailing of the events of each campaign, view the campaign's individual page. For a more detailed explanation of those worlds and places, read the Encyclopedia, where many names and nouns will be documented in a manner that will allow you to use your browser's page search function to find exactly what you are looking for.
