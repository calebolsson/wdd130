Campaigns Timeline Encyclopedia

Pre-Fracture (~2010 CE)

In the dawn of magic, humans on earth make first contact with another realm and mistake it for a digital realm. Their error eludes the public awareness, all except for a few children.

The Global Grid (~2184 CE)

When the truth of the Grid is revealed, many new adjustments must be made to accommodate the new beings that are joining humanity on Earth.

World War III (~2207 CE)

Blind to the world's cold conflicts, intellectuals leap into the cosmos. Empires rise and fall, continents are reshaped, and all of history is nearly lost to the destruction.

Aureum Spiralis (~2492 CE)

The world is born anew, and a new history is being written. Technology is rapidly returning to earth thanks to the assistance of magic, yet humanity's freedom is in peril.

Post-Rifts (~3030 CE = 0 NE)

Space-time is ripped apart, and many branching realities are created. This temporal strain is quickly reverted thanks to a team of heroes, and the damage seems to be mitigated.

Expansion (~451 NE)

A once-more flourishing humanity explores the system now within their reach, and populates it all. New factions are born, and guilds surpass nations and tribes in authority.

The Storm (~750 NE)

Outside the system, a storm rolls ever closer. Adventurures from across the planes can sense its approach as its winds ruffle the folds of their stories...
